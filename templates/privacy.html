Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we collect the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling you to filter and manage your records.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality of the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all associated expense data will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We use Google Fonts to load typefaces — this may result in a request to Google's servers when you visit the site, subject to Google's own privacy policy.

We do not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or data broker platforms.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing any concerns promptly and transparently.
